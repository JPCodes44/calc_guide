Welcome to my online math tutorials and notes. The intent of this site is to provide a complete set of free online notes and/or tutorials for anyone looking to get a better grasp on Calculus 3 and AI (Machine Learning concepts). I've tried to write the notes/tutorials in such a way that they should be accessible to anyone wanting to learn the subject regardless of whether you are in my classes or not. However it is assumed that you've got prior knowledge in the standard set of prerequisite material needed for that class. In other words, it is assumed that you know Algebra, Trig, Caclulus 1, and Calculus 2 prior to reading the Calculus 3 notes. The assumptions about your background that I've made are given with each description below.
